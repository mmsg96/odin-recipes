World's Best Lasagna

he Allrecipes community adores this lasagna recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

Ingredients

  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato producs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Make the ricotta mixture.
  3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How Long to Cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50 minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F.
Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes.
Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
